Introducing Me

Hello, friends! My name is Audrey.

A little about me. I'm a senior at Virginia Tech majoring in Political Science (ugh, politics), but my dream job is to run my own daycare which I didn't figure out until this year. I'm a big people person and I will talk your ear off if you give me the time of day... you have been warned. I love giving gifts, but I'm terrible at keeping them a secret. If you find me in front of the TV, I'm probably watching New Girl or as I like to call it "my comfort show." And while my stature may be small, I make up for it in my big personality.

Anyway, enough about me. On this wesbite you'll be able to find my hydroponics blog, a podcast I made with friends about streaming services, some helpful tips on how to stop yourself from binging all your favorite shows, and a YouTube video highlighting my favorite cruelty-free makeup products! Caring Means Cruelty Free

I admit, I am a huge animal lover and I mean all animals. I often get teased by family and friends because I'll take the extra steps (and face the fear) to catch and release a spider that got inside before I ever consider squashing it. I just don't like harming other living creatures. So, it's probably no surprise that I choose cruelty-free makeup products. To all my makeup-wearing gals and guys out there - using quality cruelty-free products doesn't have to be hard or expensive.
