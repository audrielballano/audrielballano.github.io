No Soil? No Problem!

Whether you've never heard of hydroponic gardening before or it's a familiar concept that you want to know more about or maybe try out for yourself this blog post can serve as a great resource for you! Throughout the blog I touch on the what hydropnic gardening is, the history of this method of growing crops, the benefits of hydropnics compared to traditional methods of gardening, AND at the end of the blog you'll find information on how to start your own at home hydroponic system! Caring Means Cruelty Free

I admit, I am a huge animal lover and I mean all animals. I often get teased by family and friends because I'll take the extra steps (and face the fear) of catching a spider that got inside before I ever consider squashing it. I just don't like harming other living creatures. So, it's probably no surprise that I chose cruelty free makeup products. To all my makeup-wearing gals and guys out there - using quality cruelty-free products doesn't have to be hard or expensive. Check out my video where I review some of my favorite cruelty-free products and give a little every-day makeup tutorial using those products!
